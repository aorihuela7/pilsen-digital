Vaeranda is the literary platform of the Department of Romance Languages at the University of Chicago. Here you will find links to published student work related to the Pilsen Digital project.

About Vaeranda

Vaeranda is a student publication space that celebrates creative and academic writing in Spanish, Portuguese, French, and Italian. The magazine offers a platform for students to share their work with a broader audience.
